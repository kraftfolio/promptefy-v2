AI Video Prompt Guide

The definitive guide to writing AI video prompts. Master prompt engineering for Sora, Runway, Flux, Midjourney, and every major AI video generator.

📐 The Universal Prompt Framework

Every effective AI video prompt follows a layered structure. Think of it as building a scene description from the inside out:

[Subject] + [Action/Motion] + [Setting] + [Camera] + [Lighting] + [Style] + [Technical Specs]

Example using the framework:

A young woman [Subject] walks confidently through a crowd [Action] in a rain-soaked Shibuya crossing [Setting]. Camera tracks her from behind at shoulder height [Camera]. Neon signs cast colored reflections on wet pavement [Lighting]. Film noir meets cyberpunk aesthetic [Style]. 4K, 16:9, slow motion [Specs].

🎬 Platform-Specific Tips

OpenAI Sora

  • Handles longer, narrative descriptions well
  • Responds to emotional and atmospheric language
  • Excels at realistic human motion and coherent scenes
  • Supports text-to-video up to 60 seconds

Runway Gen-3

  • Prefers shorter, focused prompts
  • Best for controlled camera movements and transitions
  • Image-to-video mode allows reference frames
  • Motion brush gives granular control over movement areas

Flux & Others

  • Photorealistic strengths — excellent for product and nature shots
  • Responds well to technical photography terms
  • Combine with img2vid workflows for maximum control

⚡ Prompt Power Words

These terms consistently improve AI video output quality:

🎥cinematic, IMAX, 35mm film
💡golden hour, volumetric, neon
📷tracking shot, dolly, crane
🎨photorealistic, editorial, macro

🚫 Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Being too vague — "A nice video of a city" gives poor results. Add detail.
  • Contradictory instructions — Don't mix conflicting styles like "minimalist and maximalist"
  • Ignoring aspect ratio — Always specify 16:9, 9:16, 4:5, or 1:1
  • Overloading with text — For Runway, keep prompts under 100 words for best results
  • No motion description — Static prompts create static videos. Describe movement!
