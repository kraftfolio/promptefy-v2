🎬 What Makes a Great AI Video Prompt?

A great AI video prompt is specific, structured, and visual. The best prompts for AI video generators like Sora, Runway Gen-3, and Flux include these elements:

  • Subject — Clearly describe who or what is in the scene
  • Camera Movement — Pan, dolly, tracking shot, crane, handheld
  • Lighting — Golden hour, neon, studio, natural, cinematic
  • Style/Mood — Cinematic, documentary, dreamy, gritty, surreal
  • Duration & Aspect Ratio — 4:5, 16:9, vertical, etc.

What are the best AI video prompts in 2026?

How do I write effective prompts for AI video generators?

Effective AI video prompts should include: 1) Subject description, 2) Camera movement (pan, zoom, tracking), 3) Lighting conditions, 4) Art style reference, 5) Duration and aspect ratio. Be specific about motion, atmosphere, and transitions for the best results.
